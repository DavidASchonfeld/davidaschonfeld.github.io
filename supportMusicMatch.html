What is Music Match?

Coming soon to the Apple App Store Near You

Listen to your favorite songs by playing Music Match. Match the song currently playing with the quiz option below. Music Match uses the downloaded songs in your Music Library to ensure that only your favorite songs are used. No internet connection needed! You can adjust the difficulty and the amount of questions asked!
