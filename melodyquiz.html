Melody Quiz

Available on the App Store on Apple devices

What is Melody Quiz?

Melody Quiz is an app game that uses the downloaded songs in your Music Library to create a personalized music quiz for you! Match the song currently playing with the quiz option below. You can adjust the difficulty and the amount of questions asked. No internet connection needed, so you can play it anywhere, even on the airplane!
